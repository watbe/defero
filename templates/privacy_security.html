Privacy Policy

Use of cookies

This website uses cookies, a common form of storing information in a browser. We use cookies in order to ensure that the form is being submitted by the correct user, anonymous or not. A randomly generated ID number is stored in the cookie. The cookie does not contain any personal information.

Access of service

By using this service, you understand that your IP address may be recorded by the server or any intermediate access points, such as network routers (i.e., university wide routers). However, this website only allows access via an encrypted connection (HTTPS), so no data or content can be viewed by intermediate access points.

It is your responsibility to ensure that your browser verifies the encrypted connection before using this service. This is usually indicated by a lock symbol by the address bar.

You agree that using this service may result in someone identifying that you use this service, however your content is transferred securely.

Records

When you look at this web site our server makes a record of your visit and logs the following information:

  • your browser’s internet address;
  • the date and time of your visit to the site;
  • the pages you accessed and documents downloaded;
  • the previous site visited;
  • the type of browser you are using; and
  • the username entered if accessing a restricted site.

We use this information for statistical purposes and for system administration tasks to maintain this service. We do not attempt to identify individuals of our regular business practices however in the unlikely event of an investigation, a law enforcement agency or other government agency may exercise its legal authority to inspect our server’s logs.

If at any stage you provide us with your email address either as part of a message or by completing a web form, we will only use your email address for the purposes for which it was provided. We will not subscribe your email address to any mailing list without your consent.
